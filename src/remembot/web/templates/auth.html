How to Access Your Knowledge Base:

  1. Open your Telegram app
  2. Go to your conversation with RememBot
  3. Send the command: /web
  4. Click the link you receive to access your data

Don't have RememBot yet? Start a conversation with @YourRemembotUsername on Telegram and send /help to get started.

What RememBot Can Store:

  • 📄 Text messages and notes
  • 🔗 URLs and web pages
  • 🖼️ Images (with text extraction)
  • 📁 Documents (PDF, Word, Excel, etc.)
  • 🎵 Voice notes and audio files
